Celestiaco

Salento IGT

Scroll Down

This wine is made for special affinities,Celestiaco is precious itself for its meaning and even for its production: in fact, this wine comes from the selection of plant-wilt best grapes.
Celestiaco is characterized by a timeless elegance, and it is never pretentious; this sweet-sweet wine never spoils in sickening: it goes along things, delighting the palate, at the end of a tasty meal.
Celestiaco is something to share, especially if paired to pleasing conversations and meditation moments or contemplative silences.
