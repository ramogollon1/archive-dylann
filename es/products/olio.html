Olio

Extra-Virgin Oil

Scroll Down

Our extra-virgin oil comes from olives we usually gather once they’re green/violet coloured (we use to call them “invaiate”); once picked the olives are brought to the a controlled temperature oil mill where they are grinded and crushed in maximum 24 hours.
The oil coming from this production is kept in steel silos for a certain time: this to collapse and eliminate impurities.
After the resting time, the oil is usually bottled without being filtered in 500 ml bottles or 2 lt cans.
Our extra-virgin oil appears limpid and in a vivid color green, it has the best of the perfumes and a really intense flavour.
