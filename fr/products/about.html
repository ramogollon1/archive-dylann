    • We are cross-cultural-polyglot agency based in Sweden, formed by a group of creative professionals in different disciplines. Our main objective is enhancing the performance of organizations all over the world. Our international team has worked with companies from different countries, including France, England, Costa Rica, Cambodia, and Russia. Our customers range include non-profit organizations, technological companies, security providers, interior design studios, tourism agencies among others.

    • It didn’t take long for Teodosio to become an expert and regarded wine taster: he’s a passionate perfectionist. Thanks to his unindisputable talent and his natural inclination to profoundly “good” and “beautiful”, Teodosio has fastly achieved important carrier-goals, such as, for example, the collaboration as selector and taster with one of the most important gourmet guides in Italy, renamed associations and academic institutions.
